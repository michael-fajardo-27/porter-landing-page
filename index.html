The problem
You outsourced your bookkeeping. But the problems didn't go away.
Whether it's a firm, a freelancer, or an in-house hire, the same frustrations keep coming up. Here's what founders tell us over and over.
01
Slow and unresponsive
You wait days or weeks for replies. Critical questions stall. Month-end momentum dies. You're paying for a service that moves slower than you do.
02
Communication is chaos
Important context lives across Slack, email, texts, and shared folders. When your bookkeeper misses something, nobody knows where the instruction originally lived.
03
They don't understand your business
Your bookkeeper manages 20 to 30 clients. They don't have the context to categorize transactions correctly or maintain schedules accurately. Nuances never make it into the ledger.
04
Output is slow, inconsistent, and wrong
Month-end takes 3 to 4 weeks. Prepaids, accruals, and depreciation schedules get missed. Nuanced transactions are booked incorrectly. Someone internal still has to review everything, defeating the purpose of outsourcing.
05
Static financials, zero insight
Your bookkeeper delivers financial statements and considers the job done. But you need more: what changed this month, should you be concerned, and how does this affect runway, hiring, or pricing? You get reports. You need clarity.
The result?
Founders end up with numbers they can't trust and a finance function that feels like a black box.

Transaction management

Every transaction, categorized and ready to review.

Bank and credit card transactions flow in automatically. Porter categorizes each one using patterns it learns from your business, flagging what needs attention and handling the rest. You see a clear "Ready" vs "Needs Review" inbox instead of an unorganized dump of data.

Schedule management

The stuff your bookkeeper keeps forgetting.

Fixed assets, prepaids, intangibles. Porter tracks every schedule, calculates monthly entries, and posts journal entries to QuickBooks automatically. No more spreadsheets. No more missed periods. No more "we'll catch up next month."

Revenue tracking

Invoices and payments, matched and tracked.

Track every invoice from issuance to payment. Porter matches incoming payments to open invoices, flags aging receivables, and keeps your revenue clean. No more chasing spreadsheets or your bookkeeper for updates.

Financial intelligence

Reports that answer the questions founders actually ask.

Every month, Porter generates a financial review that tells you what changed, what's trending, and what needs attention, with variance analysis, expense breakdown, and recommendations. Written for you, not your accountant.
